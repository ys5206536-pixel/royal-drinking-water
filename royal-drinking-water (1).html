Our Origin Story

Born on the Banks
of the Sacred Ganga

In the fertile plains of Saharsa, Bihar — where the Ganga's ancient waters have nourished civilisations for millennia — one man dared to dream of something purer.

It was the year 2018. Yuvraj Singh Rajput, walking along the mist-draped banks near Saharsa at dawn, watched the first light strike the river's surface and thought: why should the people of Bihar settle for anything less than the finest mineral water?

"The Ganga does not merely flow — she purifies. I wanted to bring that same ancient purity, bottled with modern science, to every home in Bihar."

— Yuvraj Singh Rajput, Founder

Drawing from deep-earth mineral aquifers beneath the Gangetic plains — blessed by the same ancient waters that have sustained Bihar for thousands of years — Royal Drinking Water was born.

Today, from Saharsa to every corner of Bihar, each bottle carries the legacy of the land: crisp, mineral-rich, and fit for royalty.
